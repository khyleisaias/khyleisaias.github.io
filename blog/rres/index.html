Comparison

Christianity:
nature of ultimate reality
  • god is not seen physically, but felt by it's supporters.
  • god is forgiving.
  • have 3 personas: the father, the son, and the holy spirit.

origin and purpose of the world

christianity was started in israel, and was based on the teachings of jesus of nazareth.


origin, nature and purpose of humans

the primary purpose of humans is to spread the gospel of god.


source of sacred knowledge

their source of sacred knowledge is the bible. the bible have 2 parts: the old testament and the new testament.


nature and means of salvation

you are saved by being baptized when you are a baby, and a communion when you reached teenhood.


ethics and morality

the scripture is the ultimate standard for christian ethics because god spreads his word and revelation through the scripture.


nature and purpose of the community

the church is the primary place for praising god. other churches have their own way of praising god.


nature of the afterlife

in christianity there are the concept of the purgatory, where it is like a waiting area for souls. christians also believe in hell.
